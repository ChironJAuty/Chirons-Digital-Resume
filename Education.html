Education


My education has been focused on developing skills in creative fields, beginning with a Certificate III in Animation and Games Design from the Academy of Interactive Entertainment in 2016. This program gave me a solid foundation in the basics of animation and game design, providing hands-on experience with industry-standard software and hardware. The following year, I earned a Certificate III in Childcare from ACCCO, which gave me a deep understanding of how to provide quality care and education for young children. In addition to my vocational training, I also obtained my High School Certificate from Brisbane Water Secondary College Woy Woy in 2016. This foundational education has given me the skills and knowledge to pursue my passions and excel in my chosen fields.
